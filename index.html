Frequently Asked Questions

What is your refund policy?

In most cases we will give out free test servers to start with, but if that doesn't meet your standards we have a 48hr refund policy!

How can we trust you to keep our data safe?

As a company we are really protective with our data, we do daily off-site backups and we also make sure that our security is top notch. We have a custom login system that blocks anyone from trying to SSH into our nodes and we make sure that people who have access are trusted.

What game panel do you run?

We run a modified version of Pterodactyl that also has our own custom theme!

How can I contact support?

Join our Discord or make a ticket on the billing panel! We also have a contact pop up!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and work through stripe, so your billing data is off site and not accessable by anyone, not even us!
